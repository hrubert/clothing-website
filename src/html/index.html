Blog

Blogs are still actual because the text is universal. It is a great feature for anyone who wants to share their news and stories. These blogs can contain any media content you need: galleries, sliders, lists, stand-alone images, videos, and audio. So it will be good for bloggers or just someone who wants to share some information partially. It will be also good for small articles and long-reads.

eCommerce

It isn't revolutionary to buy something online, it is so natural today, we don't even notice it. The Khaki template has eCommerce features that are needed for everyone who wants to sell something online. There is a cart, a page with billing details, products lists, of course, and single pages with products reviews. So you can use them for a store site, and simply add additions to your site to sell some merchandise, for example.

Parallaxed Backgrounds

Background images can be shown with a parallax, and the position of an image can be changed not only by just scrolling up and down but also with the cursor position and by the way you hold your device. So you can avoid the feeling of a static page and make it alive.

Swipe Side Navigations

Side menus became popular when developers started using it in their mobile apps where there is no much space on the screen. It is natural for any device right now, and can be also intuitive. And it will look amazing for a desktop version of your site too.

Smooth Animations

You can see that animations look really smooth. It is provided by the Khaki core, and it is also included in this template. Slidings, counters, buttons animations, a parallax, appearing and disappearing - these features are some kind of a language your site can talk.
